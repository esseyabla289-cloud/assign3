#report on the overall read quality and trimming results
All samples both controls and treatments(raw and trimmmed had a Phred quality score
over 30 which is great across all base pairs.

#Alignment rates across samples
For each sample had 100% mapping rate indicating that every read was alighned well.
The reads were noted as pairs and were about 99% paired. This indicates that the 
mapping was of high quality. Although there were some secondary alignments the 
numbers were very few therefore it was not a big deal.

#General patterns observed in PCA
This PCA plot shows transcriptomic variation differes between the control
samples and the treatment samples. With this plot, the treatments are 
clustered towards the PC2 which explains about 26% of the variance indicating
that some of the differences seen in this gene expression have been accounted for


#any quality concerns 
There was no major quality concerns although the samples were quite small in size.
